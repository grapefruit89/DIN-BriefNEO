# DIN 5008:2020 Technical Specification

This document outlines the structural and spatial requirements for business letters according to the German Institute for Standardization (DIN) 5008 standard.

## 1. General Page Setup
- **Format**: DIN A4 (210mm x 297mm)
- **Left Margin**: 25mm (Binding margin)
- **Right Margin**: 20mm (Minimum)
- **Font Size**: 11pt (Recommended)

## 2. Layout Forms
The main difference is the height of the letterhead, which shifts the address window.

### Form A (Small Header)
- **Header Height**: 27mm
- **Address Field Position**: Starts at 32mm from top.
- **First Fold Mark**: 87mm from top.

### Form B (Large Header) - *Standard for most business letters*
- **Header Height**: 45mm
- **Address Field Position**: Starts at 50mm from top.
- **First Fold Mark**: 105mm from top.

## 3. The Address Field (85mm x 45mm)
- **Rücksendeangabe (Return Line)**: Top 5mm. 8pt font, underlined.
- **Recipient Area**: Remaining 40mm.
- **Zone 1 (Top)**: Info/Supplementary zone (e.g., "Einschreiben").
- **Zone 2 (Bottom)**: Core address data.

## 4. Text Structure
- **Reference Block**: Contains date and location.
- **Subject Line**: Starts at 103.4mm (Form B). Must be bold. Does not include the word "Betreff:".
- **Salutation**: 2 blank lines after the subject.
- **Body**: 1 blank line after salutation.
- **Complimentary Close**: 1 blank line after body.
- **Signature**: Minimum 3 blank lines for the physical signature, followed by the typed name.

## 5. Additional Marks
- **Punch Mark (Lochmarke)**: Center of the page at 148.5mm from top.
- **Fold Marks (Faltmarken)**: Indicate where to fold for C6/5 (DL) envelopes.
